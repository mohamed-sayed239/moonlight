About Moon Light

Moon Light is a premium women's homewear manufacturing brand dedicated to designing elegant, feminine, and comfortable loungewear for modern living. Our passion lies in making every woman feel at ease and effortlessly stylish in her own space.

Established with a vision to transform homewear into an expression of beauty and comfort, our factory merges traditional craftsmanship with advanced manufacturing technologies to ensure outstanding quality in every piece we produce.

Why choose Moon Light? Because we believe that comfort should never compromise style. Every collection we craft is made with care, precision, and purpose — tailored to women who value grace and authenticity.

  • Elegance in every detail
  • High-precision production
  • Soft, skin-friendly fabrics
  • Ethical and sustainable practices

At Moon Light, we don't just manufacture homewear — we craft the feeling of home itself.
